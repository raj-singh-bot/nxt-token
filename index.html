About Us

“NXT Tokens” is a once in a lifetime opportunity for those who want to learn the technology of future and grow at a sonic speed. “NXT Tokens” is a membership platform. This platform provides a unique opportunity to all to become a part of a big community and learn about block chain technology and also earn rewards. The moto of this platform is to provide equal opportunity to each and every one who wants to learn new age block chain technology and benefit from the next boom of the century called “crypto”.

“NXT Tokens' is a venture of “NXT group”. “NXT group” consists of mainly two companies namely “NXT investments” and “NXT Tokens”. Mr. Theo Albert, a Mexico-American, is the CMD of the “NXT group”. He joined Mr. Stanley Druckenmiller’s company “Duquesne capital management”, one of the richest forex traders in the world, in 1982 as an analyst. After gaining good experience in forex market at “Duquesne capital management” he started his own company named “NXT Investments” in 1987. After 2011 the company realised the potential of crypto currency and started research and development of block chain system and crypto mining under the name of “NXT Tokens”. Understanding the huge potential of the crypto market, the company decided to enter into it and make this opportunity available to all who will be a part of the community of its new platform - “NXT Tokens”.
